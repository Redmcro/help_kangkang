# 📚 补办公室日常搞笑事件 (Add Funny Office Events)

## Rules
See `.agents/AGENTS.md`

## Assigned Files
`data/events/general.json`

## Required Reading
1. `design/_global/_schema.md` — event format + hint rules
2. `design/_global/attributes.md` — attribute keys

## Task
Add **10–15 funny office life events** (non-work, slice-of-life humor).

**Humor directions:**
- Vending machine eats your money but gives double snacks
- Someone microwaves fish in the office — war begins
- Fire drill during the most critical deploy
- Elevator small talk escalates into deep philosophical debate
- Someone's phone alarm goes off in a meeting — it's a meme song
- Office plant dies, everyone holds a funeral
- Free pizza day but only pineapple pizza left
- AC war: one side freezing, other side melting
- Someone brings their dog to office, productivity drops 50% but happiness +100%
- Printer jam at the worst possible moment — ancient office curse
- Finding a secret nap room
- Office WiFi password drama

**Rules:**
- Use `system: "general"`
- Mix good/bad/neutral — lean funny not depressing
- Some with `branch` conditions for surprise outcomes
- Some as choice events with fun options
- Descriptive hints only

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] 10–15 new events
- [ ] Valid JSON, unique IDs
- [ ] Events are genuinely funny
- [ ] No numeric hints

## When Done
Report new event IDs. Wait for review.
